About USCS Auditorium

University of Colombo School of Computing Auditorium was opened on 6 th of September 2017 due to the 50th Anniversary Celebration and Vidya Jyothi Prof. VK Samaranayake Memorial Oration. this auditorium named as Vidya Jyothi Prof. VK Samaranayake Auditorium of UCSC.
